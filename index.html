About TSA

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national organization dedicated to developing leadership, academic, and business management skills in high school and middle school students who have a strong interest in technology.

Through our programs, competitions, and activities, students gain hands-on experience in STEM fields while building valuable skills for their future careers.
